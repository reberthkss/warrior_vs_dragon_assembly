## Strategic Choices

### Scenario 1: Dragon Below 25% HP
- **Choice**: Attack with high damage (Flank) vs. Heal
- **Risk**: Potion cooldown may leave you vulnerable to Inferno
- **Reward**: Finish the dragon before it can act

### Scenario 2: Dragon at 50% HP
- **Choice**: Use Sword to stun vs. Build consistent damage
- **Timing**: Save 1 potion for emergencies
- **Balance**: Chip damage slowly or risk aggressive plays

### Scenario 3: Low HP (< 30)
- **Forced Decision**: Must use potion or lose
- **Trade-off**: Can't use potion for next 2 rounds (vulnerable window)
- **Alternative**: Try to dodge with Lance + evasion stacking
